YouCare

Overview

Clients of this daycare start-up are relying on the account managers too much and often times, the conversation gets too personal which causes too much time spent on the phone. How might we reduce interaction time so that the business can scale client service?

Solution

By giving the clients ways in which they can seek assistance and express their personal thoughts without the account managers being the first line of defense. The self-service approach will empower the clients to find the answers first, which satisfies them and the account managers as well. According to Gartner, self-service can reduce support costs by at least 25%.
